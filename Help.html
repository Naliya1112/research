How do you get started ?


If you're having trouble accessing the camera or scanning the QR code, follow these steps:

Camera Permissions

Ensure your browser has camera permissions enabled.

Hold the QR code

Hold the QR code steadily in front of the camera until it is detected

Browser Compatibility

Try using an updated browser that supports WebRTC and WebXR.
